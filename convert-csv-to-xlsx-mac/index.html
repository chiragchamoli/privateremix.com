FAQ

CSV to Excel, answered.

Will I lose quality converting CSV to Excel?

PrivateRemix preserves your content faithfully during the conversion. Text, images and layout are carried across as cleanly as the formats allow.

Why convert CSV to Excel?

CSV holds raw data with no formatting or formulas. Converting to XLSX gives you a real spreadsheet with proper columns you can style and calculate on.

How well are tables extracted into Excel?

Clearly structured tables convert cleanly into rows and columns. Very complex or scanned tables may need a quick review after conversion.

Do my files get uploaded when I convert CSV to Excel?

No. PrivateRemix runs entirely on your Mac using its own processor. Your CSV files never leave the device — there is no server to upload to, and it works fully offline.

Can I convert several CSV files to Excel at once?

Yes. Drop in a batch of CSV files and PrivateRemix converts them all locally, saving each new Excel next to its original.
