FAQ

HEIC to JPG, answered.

Will I lose quality converting HEIC to JPG?

The change is minimal. Both formats are compressed, and you choose the size-versus-quality balance — Smaller file, Balanced, or Best quality — so everyday use shows no visible or audible drop.

Why won't Windows or older apps open my HEIC files?

HEIC is an Apple-favoured format that many Windows programs, websites and older devices still don't support. Converting to a standard format fixes that everywhere.

Is JPG good for photos?

Yes. JPG is the most widely supported photo format and compresses photographs efficiently. It is a lossy format, so it is best for final images rather than repeated re-editing.

Do my files get uploaded when I convert HEIC to JPG?

No. PrivateRemix runs entirely on your Mac using its own processor. Your HEIC files never leave the device — there is no server to upload to, and it works fully offline.

Can I convert several HEIC files to JPG at once?

Yes. Drop in a batch of HEIC files and PrivateRemix converts them all locally, saving each new JPG next to its original.
