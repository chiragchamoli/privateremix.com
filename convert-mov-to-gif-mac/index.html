FAQ

MOV to GIF, answered.

Will I lose quality converting MOV to GIF?

No. GIF is a lossless format, so no image or audio detail is discarded in the conversion.

What's the difference between MOV and MP4?

Both are high quality, but MP4 is more universally supported across Windows, Android and web platforms. MOV is Apple's native container.

Why turn a GIF into a video?

GIFs are large and capped at 256 colours. MP4 video is far smaller at the same quality and plays smoothly on phones and social platforms.

Do my files get uploaded when I convert MOV to GIF?

No. PrivateRemix runs entirely on your Mac using its own processor. Your MOV files never leave the device — there is no server to upload to, and it works fully offline.

Can I convert several MOV files to GIF at once?

Yes. Drop in a batch of MOV files and PrivateRemix converts them all locally, saving each new GIF next to its original.
