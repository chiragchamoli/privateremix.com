FAQ

MP4 to GIF, answered.

Will I lose quality converting MP4 to GIF?

No. GIF is a lossless format, so no image or audio detail is discarded in the conversion.

Is MP4 the best video format?

For sharing and playback, yes — MP4 is supported by nearly every phone, TV, browser and editor, which is why it's the safe default.

Why turn a GIF into a video?

GIFs are large and capped at 256 colours. MP4 video is far smaller at the same quality and plays smoothly on phones and social platforms.

Do my files get uploaded when I convert MP4 to GIF?

No. PrivateRemix runs entirely on your Mac using its own processor. Your MP4 files never leave the device — there is no server to upload to, and it works fully offline.

Can I convert several MP4 files to GIF at once?

Yes. Drop in a batch of MP4 files and PrivateRemix converts them all locally, saving each new GIF next to its original.
